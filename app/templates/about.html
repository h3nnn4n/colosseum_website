Hi, welcome to the Colosseum!

Colosseum is a modular framework for automated tournaments between AI Agents. It was born out of passion. I wanted it to be what I wish I had during college: A place where I can test myself against others, do AI, compete and have fun.

Currently Colosseum is a functional work in progress. It is 100% automated. People can upload their agents, and the agents are automatically paired against each other in multiple competition formats. Their ratings are updated accordingly. Replays of the games can be downloaded and visualized with a renderer.

The project is fully opensource and available on github and gitea.
